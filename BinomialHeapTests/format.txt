n - количество запросов
n строк с запросами
0 heap_id - is_empty() in heap heap_id
1 heap_id x - insert(x) in heap heap_id
2 heap_id - get_min() in heap heap_id
3 heap_id - extract_min() in heap heap_id
4 heap_id i - delete element, inserted in i-th insert in heap heap_id (this element must be in this heap)
5 heap_id i x - change element, inserted in i-th insert, to x in heap heap_id (this element must be in this heap)
6 - create new empty heap
7 id1 id2 - heap[id1].merge(heap[id2])
